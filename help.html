A Guide to CABOO:

Booking an CABOO:

You can book a cab instantly from your App using RIDE NOW option. All you need is to : Firstly login into your App. If you are a first time user, you will need to CREATE AN ACCOUNT with caboo to avail caboo services.On logging in, you'll be shown cabs near your location. Next Setup your pickup location and enter your drop location and select the cab type and finally CONFIRM your booking.

Account Related Issues :

How To Create An Account:

While registering please select country and enter mobile number that you would like to use to book a ride in caboo. Next, enter the details such as name and email id to create an new account. Once you have filled al the deatils , tap REGISTER

Forgot password for my Account:

If you have forgotten the password for your Account, follow the steps given below: Click on Forgot Password? link available on the screen to reset your password. You will recieve a password reset link on your email ID provided to reset your password. Then, you can set a password.


All About CABOO Services:

Types Of Cabs Available:

Micro and Mini are the most economical and popular cab category in CABOO. In Micro and Mini category cars like Maruti Ritz, Nissan Micra, Tata Indica, Maruti WagonR, Hyundai Eon and Datum Go are available. Also there is another type Prime where the fares are higher when compared to Micro and Mini.

Seating Capacity Of Passengers:

Both Micro and Mini can seat upto 5 passengers. Children above 5Years are considered as a single passenger.
